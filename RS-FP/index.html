Final Project

Animals are living organisms that belong to the kingdom Animalia. They come in many shapes and sizes, from tiny insects to massive whales. Most animals can move independently, rely on other organisms for food, and respond quickly to their surroundings, making them a vital part of Earth's ecosystems.

They play an essential role in maintaining the balance of nature. Some animals help pollinate plants, others control pest populations, and many provide humans with companionship, labor, or resources. Protecting animal habitats is crucial for preserving biodiversity and the health of our planet.
